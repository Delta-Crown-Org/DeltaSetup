The Mission

Onboard the person correctly once. Let the ecosystem do the rest.

Delta Crown is the first full brand implementation of a cleaner operating model: identity attributes describe who someone is, groups translate that identity into access, and Microsoft 365 resources inherit that structure instead of being manually patched together after the fact.

Business Value

  • searchDelta team members show up clearly in directory searches as Delta Crown / Delta Brands people.
  • hubCorporate operators, FBCs, and support teams know where to find brand workspaces and who belongs there.
  • ruleRole, brand, department, and location changes become controlled identity updates instead of one-off permission chaos.
  • rocket_launchThe same pattern can be reused for Bishops, Frenchies, Lash Lounge, and future brands.

Repeatable Brand Pattern

Delta is the starting point, not the exception.

1. Standardize Identity

Every brand user gets consistent attributes: brand, company, department, title, role, location, manager, and support scope.

2. Standardize Groups

Dynamic security groups, M365 groups, distribution lists, and app roles are driven from those attributes.

3. Standardize Workspaces

SharePoint hubs, Teams channels, libraries, lists, and permissions are provisioned from a known pattern.

Operating Outcomes

  • paymentsLicensing and billing can roll up cleanly across the cross-tenant ecosystem.
  • keyMFA now, passkeys next, and consistent identity security across brands.
  • appsEnterprise apps can use Microsoft identity for SSO and lifecycle-managed access.
  • schemaNew brands get an operating framework instead of starting from a blank tenant.

The new brand operating system is identity-led.

Start with a clean person record. Let attributes drive groups. Let groups drive SharePoint, Teams, mail, apps, and support visibility. Then repeat the pattern for every brand.
