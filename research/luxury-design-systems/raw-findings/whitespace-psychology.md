## Key Findings

### Whitespace as Luxury Signal

**Core Principle**:
> "This is especially relevant in luxury branding, where restraint implies exclusivity."

> "Think Apple, Aesop, or Bang & Olufsen — all lean heavily on white space as a signal of confidence."

---

### Retail Spacing Patterns

**Luxury Approach**:
- Generous white space
- Creates premium quality perception
- Signals confidence
- Example brands: Apple, Aesop, Bang & Olufsen

**Discount Approach**:
- Dense layouts
- Minimal white space
- Signals accessibility and efficiency

**Key Insight**:
> "Luxury brands have long understood this principle — notice how high-end retailers use generous white space to convey premium quality, while discount..."

---

### The Confidence Signal

Whitespace communicates:
1. **Exclusivity**: Space = value
2. **Confidence**: No need to fill every pixel
3. **Quality**: Restraint implies sophistication
4. **Premium positioning**: Generous spacing = premium

---

### Application to Digital Design

**Principles transferable from physical to digital**:
- Same psychological effect
- Generous padding = luxury signal
- Breathing room = value perception
- Asymmetric spacing = editorial sophistication
