## Material 3 Shape System Overview

### Shape Scale

**Purpose**: Communicate brand personality and interaction states

**Default Scale**:
- **0dp**: Sharp corners
- **4dp**: Small corners
- **8dp**: Medium corners  
- **12dp**: Large corners
- **16dp**: Extra-large corners
- **28dp**: Full rounding (pill shapes)

### Border Radius by Component

**Cards**: 12dp (medium-large)
**Buttons**: 24dp (large, approaching pill)
**Dialogs**: 16dp (medium-large)
**Text fields**: 8dp (medium)
**Chips**: 8dp (medium)
**Navigation**: 0-16dp depending on style

### Design Philosophy

**Rounded Corners Signal**:
- Friendliness
- Approachability
- Modernity
- Playfulness
- Accessibility (softens interfaces)

**Quote from Material Design**:
> "The M3 shape system includes original shapes, a corner radius scale, and built-in shape morphing"

## Contrast with Luxury Design

### Material Design vs Luxury

| Aspect | Material Design | Luxury Design |
|--------|----------------|---------------|
| Default radius | 8-28dp | 0-4dp |
| Philosophy | Friendly, approachable | Authoritative, exclusive |
| Button shape | Pill (24dp+) | Rectangle (2-4dp) |
| Card corners | 12dp | 0-4dp |
| Visual effect | Soft, welcoming | Sharp, precise |

### Implications for Executive Presentations

**Material Design**: Designed for broad accessibility and approachability
**Luxury Adaptation**: Reduces radius 50-75% for authority and sophistication
