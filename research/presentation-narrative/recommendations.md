### Recommended Slide Structure (10 slides)

Using the **Hybrid Framework** (Raskin opening → Minto SCQ → Duarte contrast → evidence → close):

```
SLIDE 1 — TITLE (unchanged)
"A Scalable Model for Multi-Brand Operations"
→ Keep as is. Sets the stage.

SLIDE 2 — THE BIG SHIFT [Raskin Element 1]
"The Multi-Brand Opportunity Is Changing"  
→ Replaces current "The Opportunity" with a more urgent framing
→ Name the external shift: brands that systematize win; ad-hoc operations can't scale
→ This is NOT about Delta Crown yet — it's about the market

SLIDE 3 — THE COMPLICATION [Minto SCQ + Duarte "What Is"]
"Where We Are Today" (Current State Pain)
→ Merge current slides 2 (Opportunity) and 4 (Current vs. Future) into ONE slide
→ Focus on the PAIN: fragmented processes, duplicated effort, compliance risk
→ 3-4 key pain points maximum (not a data table)

SLIDE 4 — THE VISION [Duarte "What Could Be" + Raskin Promised Land]
"The Future: Unified Multi-Brand Operations"
→ The "What Could Be" — vivid picture of the outcome
→ NOT features yet — just the desired end state
→ Key metrics: speed to market, cost per brand, compliance confidence

SLIDE 5 — THE PROVEN MODEL [Raskin Magic Gifts + Lighthouse Framing]
"The Delta Crown Model™ — Proven & Ready to Scale"
→ Merge current slides 5 and 5b into ONE conceptual slide
→ Frame as the lighthouse: "We built and validated this at Delta Crown"
→ Show the model at a conceptual level (not architecture diagram)
→ Move SharePoint Hub & Spoke detail to appendix slidedoc

SLIDE 6 — LIGHTHOUSE RESULTS [Evidence + Minto Pyramid Support]
"Phase 1 Results: What We've Proven"
→ Merge current slides 6 (Efficiency Gains) and 11 (Success Metrics)
→ 3-4 KEY metrics only: time saved, cost reduced, errors eliminated, compliance improved
→ Each metric should answer "So what does this mean for scaling?"

SLIDE 7 — THE SCALE-OUT PLAN [Implementation]
"Scaling the Proven Model Across 8 Brands"
→ Merge current slides 7 (Implementation Playbook) and 10 (Scalability Roadmap)
→ 3-phase visual: Prove (✅ done) → Scale → Optimize
→ Key milestones and decision gates
→ Show decreasing cost/complexity per brand (the franchise model)

SLIDE 8 — COST & ROI [Business Case]
"Investment & Return"
→ Streamline current slide 8 (Cost Optimization)
→ Frame as investment with demonstrated returns (lighthouse data)
→ Project ROI at full scale based on proven unit economics
→ Include break-even timeline

SLIDE 9 — RISK MITIGATION [De-Risking]
"Built-in Risk & Compliance Standardization"
→ Streamline current slide 9
→ Frame risk reduction as a BENEFIT of the model, not a separate section
→ 3 key risk areas addressed + how the model solves each

SLIDE 10 — THE ASK [Duarte "New Bliss" + Call to Action]
"Strategic Recommendation: Approve Phase 2 Scale-Out"
→ Replaces current slide 12 with a more compelling close
→ Restate the Promised Land briefly
→ Clear, specific ask: what you need, by when, expected outcome
→ End with the Delta Crown vision at full scale ("New Bliss")
```

## Priority 3: Apply Anti-Pattern Fixes (MEDIUM IMPACT)

### Slide Density Audit Checklist

For each slide in the restructured deck, verify:

- [ ] **One key message** per slide (can be stated in one sentence)
- [ ] **Maximum 5 bullet points** (ideally 3)
- [ ] **Maximum 40 words** of text visible on slide
- [ ] **One chart/visual** per slide (if data is shown)
- [ ] **Clear "So What?"** — every slide answers "what should the executive do with this?"
- [ ] **20,000 ft altitude** maintained — no ground-level technical detail
- [ ] **No reading required** — slides are visual; words come from speaker

### The "Slidedoc" Leave-Behind

Create a supplementary document (separate from the presentation) containing:

1. **Detailed SharePoint architecture** (current slide 5b content)
2. **Full metrics dashboard** (current slides 6 and 11 detail)
3. **Detailed implementation timeline** (current slide 7 granular plan)
4. **Complete cost breakdown** (current slide 8 detailed tables)
5. **Risk register** (current slide 9 full risk matrix)

**Distribution strategy:**
- Send slidedoc as PRE-READ 24-48 hours before the presentation
- Reference it during the talk: "The detailed architecture is in your pre-read on page 3"
- Leave printed copies for post-meeting review

## Priority 4: Enhance Emotional Contrast (MEDIUM IMPACT)

### Adding Duarte-Style "What Is / What Could Be" Moments

Each main slide should create a micro-contrast:

| Slide | "What Is" (tension) | "What Could Be" (release) |
|-------|--------------------|-----------------------------|
| 2 (Big Shift) | Ad-hoc operations can't keep up | Systematized brands scale effortlessly |
| 3 (Current Pain) | Each brand reinvents the wheel | — (this is the "What Is" slide) |
| 4 (Vision) | — (this is the "What Could Be" slide) | Unified framework, shared intelligence |
| 5 (Proven Model) | "Could this really work?" (implicit doubt) | "We've already proven it at Delta Crown" |
| 6 (Results) | What we expected | What we actually achieved (exceed expectations) |
| 7 (Scale Plan) | Scaling seems complex | The playbook makes it simple (decreasing cost curve) |
| 8 (ROI) | The investment required | The return multiplier at scale |
| 10 (The Ask) | Leaving value on the table | Full portfolio operating as one |
