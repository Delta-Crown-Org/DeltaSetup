# Raw findings: B2B Direct Connect and Teams shared channels

Key excerpts captured from Microsoft Learn:
- B2B Direct Connect currently works with Teams shared channels.
- It does not require users to be added as guests in the resource tenant.
- Guests cannot be added to shared channels.
- Each shared channel has its own SharePoint site, and only channel owners/members can access it.
- Parent team members and even admins do not automatically have access to the shared channel site unless they are channel members.
