# Raw findings: SharePoint and Teams guest implications

Key excerpts captured from Microsoft Learn:
- SharePoint with Entra B2B integration always creates a guest account for site sharing and for file/folder sharing under that model.
- Guests can perform only basic collaboration tasks unless licensed further.
- Teams guest access requires the guest to be added to at least one Team before guest functionality becomes available.
- After a guest is added to a team, access can take up to 12 hours.
