# IBM IBV 2024 CFO Study — Raw Findings
## "6 Power Moves CFOs Must Make: Tackling Hard Truths in the Generative AI Era"
**Source:** https://www.ibm.com/thought-leadership/institute-business-value/en-us/report/2024-cfo
**Published:** September 10, 2024

## The 6 Power Moves

### 1. Strategy: Champion Tech as Core
- Technology is core to enterprise, not just an enabler
- **72% of leading CFOs**: CTOs are their most important relationship (highly important or critical)
- CFOs must elevate CTO/CIO expertise in the boardroom
- "Detailed business cases are too often yesterday's 'nice to have'"

### 2. Execution: Make Execution the Yin to Strategy's Yang
- **77% of CEOs** maintaining or increasing pace of transformational change
- **36% more leading CFOs** respond with agility to strategy changes than peers
- CFOs shifted from siloed financial/operational metrics to broader cross-organizational outlook

### 3. Investment: Show Me the ROI
- **57% of CFOs** prioritize short-term targets over long-term investments
- This increases technical debt (sacrificing maintainability for functionality)
- **30% more leading CFOs** balance cost reduction/efficiencies AND growth opportunities
- Leading CFOs "play the long game"

### 4. Risk: Determine Your Risk Tolerance, Then Place Big Bets
- **62% of CEOs** increasing risk-taking to maintain edge
- **67% more leading CFOs** continually scan for threats and opportunities vs. peers
- "Inaction or overly cautious approaches may actually pose the greatest risk"
- Replace static strategies with dynamic initiatives flexing on real-time data

### 5. Data: Make Data Your AI's Oxygen
- Only **34% of finance operations** operating or optimizing traditional AI
- AI depends on clean, timely, secure data
- Leading CFOs use data management practices **77% more** than peers
- More leaders appointed enterprise data owners and use enterprise data standards

### 6. Talent: Ignite Your Talent Revolution
- Finance experiencing shortage at intersection of technology + domain knowledge
- Leading CFOs implement employee training on data **63% more** than peers
- **64% of CEOs**: succeeding with AI depends more on people's adoption than technology itself

## Key Statistics
- **62%** of CEOs say they must rewrite their organizational playbook
- **72%** of top CEOs say competitive advantage depends on most advanced generative AI
- **39%** higher revenue growth for leading CFOs
- **77%** of CEOs maintaining or increasing pace of transformational change
