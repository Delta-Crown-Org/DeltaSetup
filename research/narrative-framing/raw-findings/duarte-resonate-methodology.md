### Key Principles
1. **Audience as Hero:** The presenter is the mentor/guide; the audience is the hero of the story
2. **Conflict and Resolution:** Use story techniques to create tension and then resolve it
3. **The Sparkline:** Alternate between "what is" (current state) and "what could be" (future state) throughout the presentation, building toward a "new bliss"
4. **Persuasive Content:** Transform presentations from information dumps into emotional journeys
