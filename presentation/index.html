Risk & Compliance Standardization

warning

High Impact / High Probability

  • Email Deliverability - Authentication framework already live; validation in final phase
  • Identity Sync Errors - Automated validation checks; phased deployment approach
error

High Impact / Low Probability

  • User Sync Failure - Monitor sync logs; fallback procedures in place
  • Collaboration Access Issues - Full access verification framework
  • Governance Policy Conflicts - Staged enforcement approach
check_circle

Low Impact / High Probability

  • User Adaptation - Training documentation; clear naming conventions
  • Sync Delays - Typical 5-10 minutes; communicate to users
  • Outlook Auto-Mapping - Manual mailbox addition available
verified

Low Impact / Low Probability

  • Minor Propagation Delays - Standard behavior; optimized for speed
  • UI Preferences - Individual Outlook customization; not blocking
  • Documentation Updates - Post-implementation maintenance

notification_important Primary Risk: User Experience During Transition

The most significant operational risk is user adaptation when accessing multiple brand environments. Mitigation strategy: Comprehensive training materials, clear operational documentation, and dedicated support during the first 30 days post-rollout.
