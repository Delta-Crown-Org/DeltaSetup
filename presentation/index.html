Risk & Compliance Standardization

🔴 High Impact / High Probability

  • Email Deliverability
    Mitigation: Authentication framework already live; validation in final phase
  • Identity Sync Errors
    Mitigation: Automated validation checks; phased deployment approach

🟡 High Impact / Low Probability

  • User Sync Failure
    Monitor: Synchronization logs; fallback procedures in place
  • Collaboration Access Issues
    Monitor: Full access verification; permission framework validation
  • Governance Policy Conflicts
    Monitor: Security policy testing; staged enforcement approach

🟠 Low Impact / High Probability

  • User Adaptation to Multi-Brand Access
    Accept: Training documentation; clear naming conventions
  • Sync Delays
    Accept: Typical sync time 5-10 minutes; communicate to users
  • Outlook Auto-Mapping Delays
    Accept: Manual mailbox addition available as workaround

⚪ Low Impact / Low Probability

  • Minor Propagation Delays
    Ignore: Standard behavior; optimized for speed
  • User Interface Preferences
    Ignore: Individual Outlook customization; not blocking
  • Documentation Updates
    Ignore: Post-implementation maintenance; not project risk

⚠️ Primary Risk: User Experience During Transition

The most significant operational risk is user adaptation when accessing multiple brand environments. Mitigation strategy: Comprehensive training materials, clear operational documentation, and dedicated support during the first 30 days post-rollout.
