  • Email Deliverability — Framework deployed. Validation in progress.
  • Identity Sync — Automated validation. Phased rollout.

High Impact / Low Probability

  • Sync Failures — Monitoring enabled. Fallback ready.
  • Access Issues — Verified framework. Full coverage.
  • Policy Conflicts — Staged enforcement. Pre-tested.

Low Impact / High Probability

  • User Training — Documentation complete. Naming conventions set.
