## How this fits with the rest of the SharePoint plan

There are two SharePoint surfaces for Delta Crown Extensions:

| Site | Audience | URL | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Crown Connection** (this one) | Franchise owners only | `/sites/CrownConnection` | Owner-to-owner channel, owner-only docs, leadership-direct comms |
| **DCE Hub** (in progress) | All staff (owners, managers, lead extensionistas, concierges, extensionistas) | `/sites/dce-hub` | Operations Manual, Services & Pricing, Membership Program, Training, etc. — the operational source of truth |

The hub is the day-to-day "how do I do my job" surface for everyone in the
studio. Crown Connection is the more private space for things that should
stay among owners — anything you wouldn't want a brand-new extensionista to
see by default.
